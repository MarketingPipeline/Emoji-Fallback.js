How to add Emoji Fallback support on your website on your website

Add this <script> Emoji-Fallback.js to your HTML document.

<script type="module">
import { emojiFallback } from 
'https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/MarketingPipeline/Emoji-Fallback.js@latest/dist/emoji-fallback.min.js';
emojiFallback();
</script>

You're done! Your website now has Emoji support on all devices!
